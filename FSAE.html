IIT MotorSports

Powertrain Team 2015-2016

From 2015 to 2016, I was leading Powertrain team of IIT Motorsport. I worked with the cross-discipline development and design student engineers throughout the team to ensure the relevant subsystems are designed with appropriate requirements, interfaces and interactions to achieve specific vehicle functions. We had an electric motor EMRAX 228 medium voltage, and we decided to go with a Limited slip-differential. We had to choose and design our parts carefully from bearings, brackets, axes to brakes and rotors.

Powertrain team members: Yong Hau Boo, Sanjay Pillai, Kohki Hori, Matthew Schindler, Vignesh Raju, Keylan Peterson
