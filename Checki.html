Anomaly detection in Health

A webapp to monitor your health

During 40 hours hackathon at ArchHack16 Washington University St. Louis, I along with a team of 3 built an app that takes personalized input data from user using a wearable device, the data can be from heart rate, temperature, to coughing pattern and tells you if you are healthy or need a checkup based on a learning algorithm

My role was to build the anomaly detection algorithm to detect abnormal heart beats, temperature, and unusual coughing patterns based on supervised learning

Team members: Haris Uddin, Amer Alsabbagh, Ali Mahmoud
